Empowering the next generation of IT leaders.

Join CUZITA and let it open a gateway for you in this exciting world of Information Technology.

Cavendish University Zambia Information Technology Association (Cuzita) is a student-run club at Cavendish Zambia dedicated to fostering IT skills and knowledge among students. We provide a platform for members to learn, network, and collaborate on exciting IT projects.

The Beauty of working hard

The key in working hard begins in one self. See the people who have gone on to achieve great things in the IT industry. Their journeys can inspire and guide you as you navigate your own career path.
